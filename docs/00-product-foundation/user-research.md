# User Research

## Target User Personas

### Persona 1: Solo Creator

**Demographics**
- Age: 22-35
- Income: $30K-$80K
- Location: Global (strong in India, Southeast Asia, Latin America)
- Tech Savvy: Moderate to High

**Content Profile**
- Platforms: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter
- Posting Frequency: 5-15 posts/week
- Content Types: Reels, Stories, Carousels, Threads
- Niche: Lifestyle, Tech, Education, Entertainment

**Current Tools**
- Canva (design)
- CapCut (video editing)
- Buffer or Later (scheduling)
- Google Sheets (content calendar)
- Notion (planning)
- ChatGPT (AI writing)

**Pain Points**
- Spends 3-4 hours/day on content
- Juggles 6-8 tools daily
- Can't afford team members
- Struggles with content consistency
- Analytics are confusing and scattered
- Platform algorithms change frequently

**Goals**
- Grow to 50K followers in 12 months
- Land brand deals worth $1K+/month
- Reduce time spent on content to 2 hours/day
- Maintain consistent posting schedule
- Understand what content works

**Willingness to Pay**
- Current: $0-$15/month on tools
- Budget: $20-$50/month for "all-in-one" solution
- Trigger: Free tier that delivers value, then upgrade for AI features

**User Journey**
1. Discovers product via YouTube tutorial or Twitter recommendation
2. Signs up for free tier
3. Connects social accounts
4. Uses AI to generate first post
5. Schedules content for a week
6. Sees analytics improve
7. Upgrades to Creator plan for AI credits

### Persona 2: Growing Creator

**Demographics**
- Age: 25-40
- Income: $60K-$150K
- Location: India, US, UK, Australia
- Tech Savvy: High

**Content Profile**
- Platforms: Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, Twitter, TikTok
- Posting Frequency: 15-30 posts/week
- Content Types: All formats including long-form video
- Niche: Business, Tech, Education, Personal Branding

**Current Tools**
- Professional editing software (Adobe, Final Cut)
- Scheduling tools (Hootsuite, Buffer)
- Analytics (native platform analytics)
- Design (Canva Pro, Figma)
- Writing (Notion, Google Docs)
- AI (ChatGPT Plus, Jasper)

**Pain Points**
- Managing multiple platforms is overwhelming
- Content repurposing is manual and time-consuming
- Team coordination (VA, designer) is messy
- Brand deals require reporting and analytics
- Can't scale without systems
- AI tools are disconnected from workflow

**Goals**
- Scale to 100K+ followers across platforms
- Hire first team member (VA or editor)
- Land brand deals worth $5K+/month
- Build systems that scale
- Maintain authentic voice while growing

**Willingness to Pay**
- Current: $50-$150/month on tools
- Budget: $50-$150/month for comprehensive solution
- Trigger: Time savings and team collaboration features

**User Journey**
1. Refers to product from creator community
2. Tries free tier, quickly hits limits
3. Upgrades to Pro plan
4. Onboards VA or team member
5. Uses AI for content repurposing
6. Builds content calendar system
7. Uses analytics for brand deal reporting
8. Becomes advocate, refers others

- Size: 5-50 employees
- Revenue: $200K-$5M
- Location: Global
- Focus: Social media management, content marketing

**Content Profile**

- Posting Frequency: 100-500 posts/week

- Team members use different tools
- Scaling requires more headcount

**Goals**

- Reduce reporting time by 75%
- Improve client retention rate
- Increase profit margins
- Build repeatable processes

**Willingness to Pay**
- Current: $500-$2000/month on tools
- Budget: $300-$1000/month for comprehensive solution
- Trigger: Client management features, reporting automation

**User Journey**
1. Discovers product via industry blog or conference
2. Signs up for trial with one client
3. Tests client portal and approval workflow
4. Migrates 3-5 clients to platform
5. Onboards team members
6. Customizes reporting templates
7. Scales to full client roster
8. Becomes case study and advocate

## User Research Methods

### Qualitative Research

1. **User Interviews** — 30-minute conversations with target users
   - 10 solo creators
   - 5 growing creators
   - 3 agencies
   - 2 brand teams
   - 5 developers

2. **Contextual Inquiry** — Observe users in their natural workflow
   - Screen recording sessions
   - Tool usage tracking
   - Pain point documentation

3. **Focus Groups** — Group discussions on specific topics
   - AI expectations
   - Pricing sensitivity
   - Feature prioritization

### Quantitative Research

1. **Survey** — 500+ responses across all personas
   - Tool usage patterns
   - Willingness to pay
   - Feature importance ranking

2. **Analytics Review** — Analyze competitor usage data
   - Feature adoption rates
   - Churn patterns
   - Upgrade triggers

3. **A/B Testing** — Validate assumptions before building
   - Pricing page layouts
   - Onboarding flows
   - Feature naming

## Key Insights

### Insight 1: Time is the #1 Currency
All personas prioritize time savings over feature count. "Save me 1 hour/day" is more compelling than "50 features."

### Insight 2: AI Expectations are High but Vague
Users expect AI to "just work" but can't articulate specific AI features. They want AI that understands their brand and audience.

### Insight 3: Pricing Sensitivity Varies by Region
Indian users are 3-5x more price-sensitive than US users. Regional pricing is essential for global adoption.

### Insight 4: Collaboration is a Growth Trigger
Solo creators upgrade when they hire their first team member. Agency features drive the highest LTV.

### Insight 5: Developers are Ecosystem Multipliers
Developer users create plugins that attract more users. They're worth investing in disproportionately.
