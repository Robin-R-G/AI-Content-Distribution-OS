# Mission Statement

## Our Mission

> Empower creators and agencies to tell better stories, reach more people, and build sustainable businesses — without drowning in tools, algorithms, or busywork.

## Who We Serve

### Solo Creators
Content creators building personal brands, thought leadership, or creative portfolios. They need to do more with less time.

### Growing Creators
Creators scaling from side hustle to full-time. They need systems, not just tools — workflows that grow with them.

### Agencies
Teams managing multiple client accounts. They need efficiency, consistency, and reporting that doesn't take days.

### Brand Teams
Internal marketing teams balancing brand voice across channels. They need governance, collaboration, and measurable impact.

### Developers
Technical users who want to customize, extend, and integrate. They need APIs, webhooks, and a plugin system.

## Our Commitments

1. **Respect creators' time** — Every feature must save more time than it costs
2. **Amplify, don't replace** — AI augments human creativity; it doesn't substitute it
3. **Stay platform-agnostic** — No favoritism; every platform gets equal support
4. **Be transparent** — Clear pricing, clear algorithms, clear data ownership
5. **Build in public** — Our roadmap is open; our development is community-driven

## What We Won't Do

- We won't sell creator data to third parties
- We won't create lock-in through proprietary formats
- We won't prioritize engagement bait over genuine content quality
- We won't charge per-post or per-platform in ways that punish growth
- We won't build features that only serve enterprise at the expense of solo creators
