## North Star Metric

### Posts Scheduled Per Active User

**Definition:** The average number of posts scheduled per weekly active user per week.

**Target:**
- Month 6: 8 posts/week
- Month 12: 12 posts/week
- Month 24: 15 posts/week

**Why This Metric:**
- Directly correlates with user value
- Predicts retention and upgrade likelihood
- Captures product stickiness
- Easy to understand and track

## Acquisition Metrics

### 1. Signups

**Definition:** Total new user accounts created.

**Targets:**
| Period | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Month 3 | 1,000 |
| Month 6 | 10,000 |
| Month 12 | 50,000 |
| Month 24 | 200,000 |

**Segments:**
- Organic (SEO, content)
- Paid (ads, sponsorships)
- Referral (word of mouth)
- Partner (integrations)

---

### 2. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Definition:** Total marketing spend / New customers acquired.

**Targets:**
| Segment | Target CAC |
|---------|------------|
| Solo Creator | $25 |
| Growing Creator | $85 |
| Agency | $450 |
| Brand Team | $1,200 |
| Blended | $50 |

**Components:**
- Paid advertising costs
- Content creation costs
- Sales team costs
- Tool and software costs

---

### 3. CAC Payback Period

**Definition:** Months to recover CAC from customer revenue.

**Target:** <6 months

**Calculation:**
```
CAC Payback = CAC / (ARPU × Gross Margin)
```

## Activation Metrics

### 4. Activation Rate

**Definition:** % of signups who complete key activation events.

**Activation Events:**
1. Complete onboarding
2. Connect first social account
3. Create first post
4. Schedule first post
5. View first analytics

**Targets:**
| Event | Target |
|-------|--------|
| Complete onboarding | 70% |
| Connect social account | 60% |
| Create first post | 50% |
| Schedule first post | 40% |
| View first analytics | 30% |

**Overall Activation:** 40% (complete all 5 events)

---

### 5. Time to First Value

**Definition:** Minutes from signup to first scheduled post.

**Target:** <5 minutes

**Measurement:**
- Track timestamp of signup
- Track timestamp of first post scheduled
- Calculate difference

**Optimization Levers:**
- Onboarding flow length
- Template availability
- AI assistance speed
- Social account connection ease

---

### 6. Onboarding Completion Rate

**Definition:** % of users who complete the onboarding flow.

**Target:** 70%

**Flow Steps:**
1. Welcome screen
2. Profile setup
3. Social account connection
4. First post creation
5. Schedule confirmation

**Drop-off Analysis:**
- Track each step completion
- Identify highest drop-off point
- A/B test improvements

## Retention Metrics

### 7. Day 1 Retention

**Definition:** % of users who return 1 day after signup.

**Target:** 40%

**Measurement:**
```
D1 Retention = Users active on Day 1 / Users who signed up on Day 0
```

---

### 8. Day 7 Retention

**Definition:** % of users who return 7 days after signup.

**Target:** 25%

**Measurement:**
```
D7 Retention = Users active on Day 7 / Users who signed up on Day 0
```

---

### 9. Day 30 Retention

**Definition:** % of users who return 30 days after signup.

**Target:** 15%

**Measurement:**
```
D30 Retention = Users active on Day 30 / Users who signed up on Day 0
```

---

### 10. Monthly Churn Rate

**Definition:** % of paying customers who cancel per month.

**Targets:**
| Segment | Target |
|---------|--------|
| Free users | 15% |
| Solo Creator | 8% |
| Growing Creator | 5% |
| Agency | 2% |
| Enterprise | 1% |

**Calculation:**
```
Churn Rate = Customers Lost / Customers at Start of Period
```

---

### 11. Customer Lifetime (LTV)

**Definition:** Average months a customer stays subscribed.

**Targets:**
| Segment | Target |
|---------|--------|
| Solo Creator | 12 months |
| Growing Creator | 18 months |
| Agency | 36 months |
| Enterprise | 48 months |

**Calculation:**
```
LTV = 1 / Churn Rate
```

## Revenue Metrics

### 12. Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)

**Definition:** Total predictable monthly revenue.

**Targets:**
| Period | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Month 6 | $5,000 |
| Month 12 | $50,000 |
| Month 24 | $200,000 |

**Components:**
- Subscription revenue
- AI credit packs
- Template sales
- Plugin commissions

---

### 13. Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)

**Definition:** MRR × 12

**Targets:**
| Period | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Month 6 | $60,000 |
| Month 12 | $600,000 |
| Month 24 | $2,400,000 |

---

### 14. Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)

**Definition:** Total revenue / Total paying users.

**Targets:**
| Segment | Target |
|---------|--------|
| Solo Creator | $5/month |
| Growing Creator | $12/month |
| Agency | $36/month |
| Brand Team | $150/month |
| Blended | $12/month |

---

### 15. Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Definition:** Total revenue expected from a customer over their lifetime.

**Targets:**
| Segment | Target |
|---------|--------|
| Solo Creator | $180 |
| Growing Creator | $960 |
| Agency | $18,000 |
| Brand Team | $48,000 |

**Calculation:**
```
LTV = ARPU × Customer Lifetime
```

---

### 16. LTV:CAC Ratio

**Definition:** Customer lifetime value / Customer acquisition cost.

**Target:** >3:1

**Benchmarks:**
| Ratio | Assessment |
|-------|------------|
| <1:1 | Unsustainable |
| 1:1-2:1 | Marginal |
| 2:1-3:1 | Acceptable |
| 3:1-5:1 | Good |
| >5:1 | Excellent |

---

### 17. Free-to-Paid Conversion Rate

**Definition:** % of free users who upgrade to paid plans.

**Target:** 5-8%

**Measurement:**
```
Conversion Rate = Paying Users / Total Users × 100
```

**Optimization:**
- Value demonstration before paywall
- Feature gating at right moments
- Upgrade prompts at high-engagement moments
- Trial periods for premium features

## Net Promoter Score (NPS)

### 24. NPS Score

**Definition:** Likelihood of users recommending the product.

**Target:** 60+

**Scale:**
| Score | Category |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | Promoters |
| 7-8 | Passives |
| 0-6 | Detractors |

**Calculation:**
```
NPS = % Promoters - % Detractors
```

**Benchmarks:**
| Score | Assessment |
|-------|------------|
| <0 | Poor |
| 0-30 | Needs improvement |
| 30-50 | Good |
| 50-70 | Excellent |
| >70 | World-class |

---

### 25. Time to Value (TTV)

**Definition:** Time from signup to first "aha moment."

**Target:** <5 minutes

**Aha Moments:**
1. First AI-generated caption that impresses
2. First scheduled post going live
3. First analytics showing engagement
4. First time saving vs manual process

**Measurement:**
- Track user journey events
- Identify time of aha moment
- Calculate average TTV
